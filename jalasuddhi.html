General information


The product plays main role in water purification / treatment plants.

Completely chemical free product and helps in environmental compliance.

It helps in ease of operation to achieve the end results.

Low maintenance cost.

High efficient end results.

Plays vital role in breaking and separation of emulsions, hydrocarbons, organic & inorganic compounds.


Composition of the products


Water cleaning probiotics with macro and micro nutrients produced from cow based natural resources.

Product application


To treat industrial Effluents, Sewage water,Water bodies like ponds, Lakes.

Direction for use


  • Water pond / Lake application: 10 litres mixed with 30 litres of water and spray in the lake per acre with 10 feet depth every fortnight. The frequency may vary depends on the present water quality status and other influential contaminant inputs and other applicable factors associated with.
  • ETP application: For factory effluents it depends on the industry, BOD, COD AND TSS level and the type of treatment plant. No chemical need to be added except for flocculation for the industrial effluents like lime, etc.In Aeration tank apply first week 1 litre for every 10000 litres daily subsequently, 1 week apply 500 ml in Aeration tank. After clarifier transfer the bottom sludge to Aeration. This will transfer the Probiotics to Aeration tank. After sand and carbon filtration back wash the water and send it to collection tank. For septic tank initial charge 10 litres for the first time and followed by 1 litre charge every month. For sewage plant apply initially 1 litre for every 10000 litres sewage in collection tank once in a month.
 

Benefits of usage


Digest and break down the unwanted impurities, waste, balance the BOD, COD,TSS content in the water to improve the water quality.

  • Probiotic is a generic term, and products can contain yeast cells, bacterial cultures, or both that stimulate microorganisms capable of modifying the gastrointestinal environment to favour health status and improve feed efficiency.
  • Mechanisms by which probiotics improve feed conversion efficiency include alteration in intestinal flora, enhancement of growth of non-pathogenic facultative anaerobic and gram positive bacteria forming lactic acid and hydrogen peroxide, suppression of growth of intestinal pathogens, and enhancement of digestion and utilization of nutrients.
