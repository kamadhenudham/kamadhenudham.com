“Matsyavriddhi” – Aquaculture solution

 

General information

This product is made produced chemical free as an aid for aquaculture industry mad out of probiotics with macro and micro nutrients produced from cow based natural resources.

Composition of the products

The product consists of probiotics with macro and micronutrients produced from cow based natural resources.

Product application

Aquaculture industry solution – As an aid for the disease prevention and improve the favourable conditions for safe growth of shrimps / fish.

Direction for use

  • First application of product – 10 Litres recommended per acre and for water bodies of about 3.5 to 4 feet deep.

  • Subsequent applications includes10 Litres per acre every for 10 days till the date of socking completion 2 months.

  • From 3rd month onwards ,10 Litres  per acre on every 15 days interval ,till the time of harvest.

  • Helps in controlling and balancing the environmental condition and thereby maintaining favourable equilibrium living medium and thus improving consistent and stable growth.

Benefits of usage

Helps in controlling and balancing the environmental condition and there by maintaining favourable the equilibrium living medium improving consistent and stable growth.

Product Technical profile facts

  • It increases the growth of algae, weeds and small worms in the pond, thus increasing the food availability to fishes.

  • The only precaution is that fresh water must be added to the ponds at frequent intervals. Otherwise, the growth of algae, weeds and other organisms will compete with the fish for the available soluble oxygen in water.

  • Alternatively, mechanical agitators can also be used to increase the oxygen content in the water. In ten months’ time each fish will grow to a weight of about 2 to 3 kgs.

  • With reduced death rate of small fingerlings and increased weight of marketable fish, the fisheries became more profitable.
