General information


Plant growth promoting probiotics. with macro and micro nutrients produced from cow based natural resources.


Composition of the products


Water cleaning probiotics with macro and micro nutrients produced from cow based natural resources.

Product application


Agriculture, plantations – For sustainable growth of plants enhancing stability, increasing immunity against disease & there by enhancing consistent and fast growth of plants.

Direction for use


  • Spray system 3% solution was found to be most effective compared to the higher and lower concentrations investigated. Three litres of product to every 100 litres of water is ideal for all crops. The power sprayers of 10 litres capacity may need 300 ml/tank. When sprayed with power sprayer, sediments are to be filtered and when sprayed with hand operated sprayers, the nozzle with higher pore size has to be used.
  • Flow system The solution of product can be mixed with irrigation water at 50 litres per hectare either through drip irrigation or flow irrigation Seed/seedling treatment 3% solution of product can be used to soak the seeds or dip the seedlings before planting. Soaking for 20 minutes is sufficient. Rhizomes of Turmeric, Ginger and sets of Sugarcane can be soaked for 30 minutes before planting.
  • Seed storage 3% of product solution can be used to dip the seeds before drying and storing them.

    • Periodicity:

  • Pre flowering phase: Once in 15 days, two sprays depending upon duration of crops.
  • Flowering and pod setting stage: Once in 10 days, two sprays.
  • Fruit/Pod maturation stage: Once during pod maturation.
 

Benefits of usage


  • Growth promoting agent, controlling and balancing the environmental anti-microbial function and improving consistent and stable growth.
  • It increase the immunity, the plants will be able to resist the pests and disease caused.
  • It has macro and micro nutrients which are the basic needs for the growth and healthy development of the plants.
  • The natural nutrients presence regulate the growth of the plants.
  • This improve and enhance the yield.


Product Technical profile facts


The main applications in which the product has accelerated and performed are as follows:

  • Due to the application of this product, the system of photosynthesis gets activated which helps in improving the biological efficiency, which in turn increase thee production of metabolites.
  • The stem will have a chance to produce side shoots which have the capacity of carrying maximum productivity. The branching will be very high when compared to the fertilizers.
  • This would improve in rooting. It becomes dense and profuse which helps in being fresh for a long period of time and which helps in the maximum nutrient intake.
  • The thin film formation on the leaves and stems of the crops will decrease the evaporation of water in the plants. The roots developed by the plants will have the capability to withstand long dry periods.
