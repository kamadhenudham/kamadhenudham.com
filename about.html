About

Kamadhenudham

Kamadhenudham markets the unique products under the brand “Kamadhenudham” that has been thoroughly researched and developed based on Vedic literature to provide respective clients with an affordable, efficient and cost-effective end results.

We will be able to develop, design, build and install tailor-made processes for specific client that incorporates our technology in providing the process benefits to the client.

The formulation of products specifically developed for a client’s application has shown to provide significant benefits for the client.

Currently our products serve and a solution for Water treatment, Agriculture, Aquaculture & Poultry.

Products are basically probiotics with macro and micronutrients produced from cow based natural resources and no added chemicals. Refer the product profile files for more information. The products are sourced from the approved goshala’s located at various location.
